our famous dishes

The aromatic brilliance of rich Indian spices are blended into every recipe that we prepare.What makes us special is that we are globalising the flavour of our local biryanis ,tiffins and bringing you a menu.
Biryani Bliss: Prepare your taste buds for a symphony of flavors as Happening takes you on a gastronomic journey through the heart of South India.The biryanis, boast an exquisite blend of aromatic spices, fragrant Basmati rice, and succulent meat or vegetable choices. Each forkful promises to transport you to the vibrant streets and kitchens of our region, where the art of biryani-making is steeped in tradition and passion.
Tantalizing Tiffins: Beyond the biryanis, Happening offers an array of delectable tiffins that redefine the art of breakfast and brunch. From the feather-light idlis served with an array of chutneys to the crisp dosas that delight with every bite, the tiffin menu is a celebration of South India's rich culinary heritage. The skilled chefs at Happening have mastered the delicate balance of textures and flavors, creating a tiffin experience that is nothing short of extraordinary.
Must-Try Recommendations:
Chicken & Cashew Nut Biryani: Savor the unique blend of spices and the perfectly cooked meat or vegetables.
Kodi Kura Chitti Gaare: Experience a medley of tiffins, each with its own distinct taste and charm.
In conclusion, Happening stands as a testament to South India's culinary prowess. Whether you are a seasoned connoisseur or a newcomer to the world of South Indian flavors, this establishment promises a dining experience that transcends the ordinary.

our history

In the bustling city of Toronto, there stood a restaurant that had become the talk of the town.Established in 2020, "Happening" quickly became famous for its delectable North and South Indian tiffins and biryanis.Happening is not just a restaurant; it is an experience that transports diners to the heart of India with every bite.The restaurant's ambiance perfectly blends modern aesthetics and traditional charm. The walls, adorned with vibrant murals depicting scenes from Indian streets, set the stage for a gastronomic adventure.Breakfast at Happening is a delight, with steaming hot idlis and crispy dosas served alongside rich, aromatic filter coffee. For lunch, various biryanis like Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, the Lucknowi Chicken Biryani, and the aromatic Chettinad Lamb Biryani.As the years passed, Happening continued to innovate, introducing new dishes while staying true to its roots.
