About

Our Famous Dishes

The aromatic brilliance of rich Indian spices are blended into every recipe that we prepare.What makes us special is that visitors can taste and feel the true flavour of our local biryanis. The promise is to transport you to the vibrant streets and kitchens of our region, where the art of biryani-making is steeped in our tradition and passion.

Tangdi Kabab is a popular appetizer or snack, often served with mint chutney or a squeeze of lime for added zest.The marinade for Tangdi Kabab usually includes ingredients like yogurt, ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, garam masala, and chili, among others. This blend of spices not only adds depth to the flavor but also gives the chicken a vibrant and appetizing color.

Our History

"In the bustling city of Toronto, there stood a restaurant that had become the talk of the town.Established in 2020, "Happening" quickly became famous for its delectable North and South Indian biryanis.Happening is not just a restaurant; it is an experience that transports diners to the heart of India with every bite.The restaurant's ambiance perfectly blends modern aesthetics and traditional charm. The walls, adorned with vibrant murals depicting scenes from Indian streets, set the stage for a gastronomic adventure. For lunch, various biryanis like Hyderabadi Dum Biryani, the Lucknowi Chicken Biryani, and the aromatic Chettinad Lamb Biryani.As the years passed, Happening continued to innovate, introducing new dishes while staying true to its roots".
